APEX'19 SUMMIT & AWARDS

February 27, 2019 - Mumbai

India's Premier Investor - Entrepreneur Interface saw the participation of the Who's Who of the Indian Private Equity & Venture Capital ecosystem. The Summit saw leading top professionals from PE-VC firms and Advisory Firms throw light on the latest trends affecting transactions - PE, VC and M&A - in the current environment.

The APEX'19 Awards recognized the leading PE and VC firms - for transactions in the year 2018 - across 14 categories. The Awards Dinner provided a great opportunity for advisory professionals and entrepreneurs to catch up & network with top PE-VC Fund Managers in a relaxed setting.

Presenting Sponsor

Universal EB5 USA Services facilitates access to eligible, high-quality EB-5 investments for individuals and families seeking to emigrate and capitalize on an opportunity for prosperity and success unique to the United States. The company employs a team of industry professionals in the EB-5 industry to ensure that each of its representative projects strictly follows a prudent due diligence process for selection in terms of investment, with a particular focus on ensuring Project Completion, Job Creation, and Investment Security.
