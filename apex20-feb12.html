APEX 2020 SUMMIT & AWARDS

India's Premier Investor - Entrepreneur Interface

March 17, 2020 - Mumbai

The Venture Intelligence Apex Summit sees the participation of the Who's Who of the Indian Private Equity & Venture Capital ecosystem. The Summit features top professionals from PE-VC firms and Advisory Firms throwing light on the latest trends affecting transactions - PE, VC and M&A - in the current environment.

The APEX Awards recognizes the top PE and VC firms across multiple investment categories. The awards see fierce competition among firms to win the coveted Apex Award.
